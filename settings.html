ABOUT

WELCOME, DIESEL.

Six years ago there was nothing here — no crew, no reputation, no name that meant anything on any board. It was built one closed job at a time: one contact trusted, one node secured, one mission signed off after another, with nobody handing anything over for free. There were stretches where the whole operation nearly folded, and stretches where it grew faster than anyone could track. Through all of it, the same rule held — answer to no one, close every job clean, and let the results speak on the board.

What stands now is what that climb built: 750 missions closed, a crew of twelve reporting straight to this node, and a network of over 2.4 million watching every move. This is DIESEL — started from zero, now running the board instead of waiting for a seat at it.
